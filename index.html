About

I’m a doctor with a deep passion for technology and teaching, blending my medical expertise with programming to make learning engaging and accessible. After completing my MBBS in 2023, I’ve been running a YouTube channel where I teach medical topics, especially ECG interpretation. As an AHA-certified BLS and ACLS provider, I strongly believe in the power of education to uplift and empower others. My personal philosophy is simple: "The more you give, the more you get." I’m always eager to share knowledge, whether through teaching or coding projects. With a love for gaming, music, and constant innovation, I approach problem-solving creatively, always excited about exploring the intersection of medicine and technology.
